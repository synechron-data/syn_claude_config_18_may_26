Bulk-select and delete multiple Claude conversations at once from the sidebar.

Navigate to claude.ai and click the ☑ Bulk Select button at the bottom-right corner of the page to begin.

  1. Go to claude.ai
  2. Click ☑ Bulk Select (bottom-right)
  3. Check the conversations to delete
  4. Click 🗑 Delete N and confirm
  5. Page reloads automatically when done
